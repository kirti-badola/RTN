Have questions?

How quickly can I get a robot delivered?

Delivery usually takes 2–4 weeks, depending on your location and customization needs.

Can I lease instead of buying?

Yes. We offer flexible monthly and lease-to-own plans to fit your budget and deployment scale.

What do I need to run the robot at my business?

Just Wi-Fi, standard power, and basic floor space. No special setup or infrastructure required.

Can I customize the robot’s appearance and voice?

Absolutely. Add your branding wrap, custom voice lines, and even celebrity-style voice cloning.

Do the robots integrate with my POS or CRM system?

Yes. RTN ROBO OS supports POS, CRM, hotel PMS, and other major system integrations via RTN Smart.

What if I need support or updates?

All robots come with OTA (over-the-air) updates, remote diagnostics, and dedicated support.

Is there a free trial or pilot program?

Yes. You can start with a 15-day free trial of the RTN Cloud dashboard and AI Host features.
